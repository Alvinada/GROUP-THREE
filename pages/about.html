Advocating for Change

Our commitment to teen parents below the age of 18.

The Problem We Address

Teen parents face immense hurdles, including school dropout, social stigma, and discrimination. Many experience domestic violence and lack the legal awareness to protect themselves. SHRO exists to fill the gap where there is no central digital platform for rights education and confidential reporting.

Our Vision

A society where every young parent is empowered, protected, and given equal opportunities to build a better future.

Target Audience

We serve pregnant teenagers, young fathers and mothers (14-25), and collaborate with social workers and legal aid organizations.
